Guidelines for students for Anand Summer School

  • Anand Summer School will be held on the online platform
  • Create a Gmail Id for registration on Google Form. If you already have one, then use it.
  • After the registration on the Google Form, you received your registration confirmation message and email.
  • Eligibility -
  • > Open for school students from classes 10th to 12th across (11th and 12th only Science students) the country, from any school and any board.
  • Attendance-
  • > There should be at least 70% of attendance according to that E-certificates and award will be provided.
  • Courses-
  • > There are 18 courses available here as per your choice you can choose any of them.
  • > These courses will enhance your skills and it will enrich your perspective areas and will be helpful for your future use.
  • > The Registration fees of each course available over here is Rs-50/.
  • > In case you are interested in two courses the charge will be Rs-75/.
  • Registration-
  • > Students are required to register themselves on the website.
  • > Register yourself on time.
  • > All important and related information will be provided on the website and via email.
  • Awards/Cash Prizes –
  • > The best student will be selected by the course coordinator.
  • > The top students will get the certificate and prize in the Award Ceremony.
  • > For a better experience with the Anand Summer School, you will don't miss any class
  • > In case of any query/confusion, kindly give a call to the coordinators.
